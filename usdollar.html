About the US Dollar

Overview

The United States dollar (USD) is the official currency of the United States and its territories. It is the world's primary reserve currency, and it is used in most international transactions. The symbol for the dollar is $.

History

The US dollar was introduced in 1792 based on the Spanish dollar and other international currencies of the time. Today, the Federal Reserve is responsible for issuing and regulating the supply of dollars.

Key Facts

  • Symbol: $
  • Currency code: USD
  • Central bank: Federal Reserve
  • First issued: 1792
  • Used in: United States, U.S. territories, and some other countries
