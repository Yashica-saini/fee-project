  1. Your account was subject to restriction or closure for another reason

What is domain impersonation?

Domain impersonation is a type of internet fraud. Fraudsters pose as a legitimate business by creating a fake website or email domain in order to steal information from users or defraud them.

We urge our customers to remain vigilant against websites and email domains that may impersonate the converter.If you’re not sure whether a service is legitimate, be discerning and exercise caution.
